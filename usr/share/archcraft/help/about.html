About

Archcraft is a minimal and resource friendly Linux distribution, Which is based on Arch Linux.

Archcraft is very lightweight and it can run under 500MB of memory. It only uses window managers and a few lightweight applications.
Archcraft has two window managers, openbox (stacking wm) and Bspwm (tiling wm). Openbox is the default window manager.
Flat and pastel colors, the consistency among different UI elements and various themes for each WM make Archcraft look and feel very unique, clean, minimal and stunning.
It has built-in support for AUR, Snaps, Flatpak. Which means, despite being lightweight and beautiful, It's powerful too and it gives you the power to do whatever you want on your Linux PC.
